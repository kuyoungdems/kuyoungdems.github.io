Our Mission

We work to educate people in the community (both on and off campus) about current issues, get students involved in the legislative process, and further the goals and ideologies of the Democratic party. Our primary goal is to increase civic engagement through education, volunteer work, and training. We work in conjunction with Kansas Young Democrats, Young Democrats of America, and the Kansas Democratic Party.
